About the Carbon Credit System

Our Mission

The Carbon Credit System is designed to encourage industries to offset their carbon emissions by supporting farmers who plant trees. This initiative promotes environmental sustainability and provides financial benefits to farmers.

How It Works

- Farmers plant trees and submit proof of plantation.
- Government officers verify the proof and issue carbon credits.
- Industries can purchase carbon credits from farmers to compensate for their emissions.
- Transactions are securely handled using Ethereum-based payments.
